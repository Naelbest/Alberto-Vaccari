So, how did he start photography?

He started photography in 2009, after leaving his home country (Italy); at the beginning it was just to show his parents and friends where he has been as well as to keep track of his experiences.

Later on, he found myself fascinated by photography itself, not only as a mean to convey information but also feelings and sensations. Slowly it became somehow part of his daily habits and now he would not imagine himself without a camera around.
